2020 → 2024

Where did we leave off?

Last time BEW met in person was:

→ February 26, 2020!

What was even going on back then?

This was happening.

Jan 30: First recorded instance of person-to-person spread of the 2019 Novel Coronavirus in the U.S. (total cases = 7). Feb 6: First novel coronavirus related death recorded in the US. Feb 11: WHO announces the official name for the disease: “COVID-19”

This guy was president.

He was impeached (for the first time) and acquitted Feb 5. We were still 9 months from the election, and 11 months from the Jan 6 attack...

This guy was the leading challenger.

Won most votes in the Iowa caucus on Feb 3.

Other notable milestones...

  • Feb 14: Sonic the Hedgehog opened on Valentine's Day and would gross $148MM
  • Feb 9: Parasite is the first non-English film to win the Best Picture Oscar
  • Feb 24: Memorial service for Kobe Bryant held
  • Feb 29: US and Taliban sign deal to end 18-year war in Afghanistan
  • Feb 2: The Chiefs beat SF in the Super Bowl LIV (54). Well, some things haven't changed :)
