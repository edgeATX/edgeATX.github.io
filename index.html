About Us

We are an Austin meetup about all things web+mobile. We are language/framework agnostic, and we cover different topics each month, with a focus on front end technology.

We meet downtown at Capital Factory at 7pm on the 3rd Thursday of each month, and we normally head a block over to Buffalo Billiards for socializing after each meetup.

Events are livestreamed on Facebook, and you can follow @EdgeATX for news and updates.
