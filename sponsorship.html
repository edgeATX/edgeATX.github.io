Sponsorship Opportunities

We typically ask for $150 to cover the cost of pizza. Any remainder is used to offset some of the costs of running the meetup. You're welcome to provide more or supply different food/drinks so long as it complies with Capital Factory's rules. This means liquor is OK as long as it is prepackaged in cans or bottles. Open liquor is also possible, but you must provide a licensed bartender.

In return, you get:

  • A thank you credit in the Bleeding Edge Web segment.
  • A few minutes to introduce your company.
  • A listing on our meetup page as a sponsor.
  • A place to hand out swag.
  • A tax write off?

This is a great opportunity to create some local awareness, find potential employees, give back to the community, and meet new people.

Alternative Sponsorship Opportunities

We've had other sponsors provide give-away items from books to gadgets. If you are interested in sponsoring a give-away, let us know!
