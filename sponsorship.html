Sponsorship Opportunities

To sponsor a meetup, or if you have any questions about sponsoring, please email us.

We ask for $250 to cover the cost of food and drink (we typically feed 80+ attendees). We handle all of the ordering and delivery logistics. Remaining money, if any, is used to offset the recurring costs of running the meetup (e.g. meetup.com fees).

Alternately, you are welcome to supply your own food/drinks instead, so long as it complies with Capital Factory's rules. This means alcohol is OK as long as it is pre-packaged in cans or bottles. Open liquor is also possible, but you must provide a licensed bartender.

In return, you get:

  • Your name in front of every person who receives our invitation emails (~2,400 as of February 2017, mostly developers in Austin)
  • A thank you slide and mention during the Bleeding Edge Web presentation
  • A chance to address the group in person, if desired
  • A listing on our meetup page as a sponsor
  • An opportunity to hand out swag, sign people up, etc.
  • A tax write off?

This is a great opportunity to create some local awareness, find potential employees, give back to the community, and meet new people.

Alternate Sponsorship Opportunities

We've had sponsors provide giveaway items from books to stickers to physical gadgets. If you are interested in providing swag or giveaway items, let us know!
